Payments simplified. Requests amplified.

Core Features

1. Payment Handling

  • QR Code Payments:

    Generate dynamic QR codes and scan for instant payments.

  • Request Payment Links:

    Send auto-expiring payment links via SMS, email, WhatsApp, or social apps.

  • Split Payments:

    Split bills among multiple payers and track partial payments in real time.

2. Wallet & Account Management

  • Multi-Currency Wallet:

    Hold multiple currencies including crypto.

  • Instant Transfers:

    Send/receive money instantly to bank accounts or other Zep POS users.

  • Transaction History & Analytics:

    Visual dashboards with real-time stats and trends.

3. POS Features

  • Inventory Management:

    Sync stock levels automatically with sales.

  • Invoicing & Receipts:

    Generate digital receipts instantly and share via QR code or email.

  • Loyalty Programs:

    Track points, rewards, and discounts automatically.

  • Offline Mode:

    Accept payments without internet; syncs later.

4. Security & Compliance

  • End-to-End Encryption:

    All transactions and payment links are encrypted for security.

  • Biometric Authentication:

    Authorize transactions with fingerprint or facial recognition.

  • Fraud Detection AI:

    Real-time monitoring for suspicious transactions.

Futuristic Design & UX

  • AI-Powered Dashboard:

  • Minimalist, Neon-Inspired Interface:

  • Voice Commands:

    Request payments instantly via voice commands.

  • Smart Notifications:

    Receive payment reminders, low stock alerts, and customer highlights.
